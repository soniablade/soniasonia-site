Hypergraphics

Interactions of humans with the earth’s surface show patterns on much larger scale and not only in visual terms. They reveal how we interact with the land beneath us, creating intentional and incidental forms. Often they reveal social behaviourisms. relevant examples are huge disposal sites for uranium, photo calibration targets (that helped develop the efficiency of drones) or mystic artefacts such as the Stonehenge prehistoric monument.

In this project, we had to choose a hectare of land in the Far East and figure out what to do with this hectare. I have chosen a hectare not far from Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky, my idea was an electronic music festival, the main reference was the Dutch festival dekmatel and our outline, which ceased to take place in 2016. I made a plan for this festival, dates, places, scenes, artists, tickets. The result of the project are posters and videos.
