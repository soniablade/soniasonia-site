Barter

Barter is an attempt at finding balance between private and public. This performance uses interaction between strangers to achieve that goal To participate in the All to Chamount competition, it was necessary to gather people and come up with a reason for gathering, as well as make a visual diplomat. Lesya Kuranova, Vasily Dyakonov, Masha Andrianova and I came up with the performance-game “Barter” and held it on February 16. Flyers and a huge number of posters were made as visual diplomats.
